Pilav Recipe

Home

Description

Turkmen pilav is a traditional rice dish made by cooking rice together with meat, onions, carrots, and aromatic seasonings. It is known for its rich flavor, and important place in Turkmen cuisine

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Meat
  • Onions
  • Carrots
  • Oil
  • Water
  • Garlic
  • Seasonings mix

Steps

  1. Heat oil and cook the meat until lightly browned.
  2. Add chopped onions and carrots, then cook until softened.
  3. Pour in water, season, and let the meat simmer until tender.
  4. Add the rice and cook until the liquid is absorbed and the rice is fully done.
