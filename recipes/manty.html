Manty (Steamed Dumplings) Recipe

Home

Description

Turkmen manty are traditional steamed dumplings usually filled with minced meat, onions, and simple seasonings. They are a hearty and well-loved dish in Turkmen cuisine, often served hot with sour cream, butter, or sauce.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Minced beef
  • Onion
  • Potatoes (optional)
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Prepare the dough by mixing flour, water, and salt, then let it rest.
  2. Make the filling with minced meat, chopped onions, and seasonings.
  3. Roll out the dough, cut it into squares, and place filling in the center of each piece.
  4. Fold and seal the dumplings, then steam them until fully cooked.
